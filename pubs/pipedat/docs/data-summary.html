a56e753b Timeline of COVID-19 in Canada The Timeline of COVID-19 in Canada (CovidTimelineCanada) is intended to be the definitive source for data regarding the COVID-19 pandemic in Canada. In addition to making available the ready-to-use datasets, this repository also acts as a hub for collaboration on expanding and improving the availability and quality of COVID-19 data in Canada. This repository is maintained by the COVID-19 Canada Open Data Working Group and is one component of the What Happened? COVID-19 in Canada project. - 2023-05-16 67 Berry et al. (2021)

7fe284e4 Native Land Digital Native Land is an app to help map Indigenous territories, treaties, and languages.
